Unique Chemical & Ion Exchange Process

Our water conditioner uses a proprietary chemical and ion exchange process to effectively neutralize the scale-forming properties of hard water minerals. Unlike traditional softeners that remove these minerals, our technology re-engineers their structure, preventing them from adhering to surfaces.

This unique action ensures that beneficial minerals are retained in your water for health, while all scale-related problems are eliminated.

SCALE FREE TANK

The SCALE FREE TANK is a floating device containing premium polyphosphate media. As it gently floats in your water storage tank, the polyphosphate slowly dissolves, releasing a safe and controlled dose into the water.

This polyphosphate bonds with calcium, magnesium, and iron ions, preventing them from combining into hard mineral scale. It also forms a microscopic protective film on tank walls, pipes, and connected appliances stopping scale before it starts.

When the polyphosphate is depleted, simply refill the device with fresh media, making it reusable and long-lasting.

Key Features & Benefits

Prevents Scale Formation

Polyphosphate stops calcium, magnesium, and iron from forming hard deposits in tanks and pipes.

Protective Film

Forms a microscopic barrier on surfaces to prevent scale adhesion and corrosion.

Reusable Design
